Welcome to Our Research Group

The Computer Vision and Robotics Group, part of the Archimedes Research Unit at Athena RC, is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of computer vision and robotics through cutting-edge research.

We are committed to advancing state-of-the-art research in developing advanced deep learning techniques, such as continual and incremental visual learning, harnessing large-scale unlabeled data to refine visual data representations, and enhancing their applicability across various tasks. We also explore neuromorphic algorithms for visual motion perception, advance multimodal machine learning and geometric deep learning, and integrate tensor methods for higher-order learning in multimodal and multiway data.
